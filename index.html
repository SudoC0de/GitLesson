How to cook Macaroni & Cheese

Ingredients

  • 2 Cups Dry Elbow Macaroni
  • 2 Cups Heavy Whipping Cream
  • 2 Cups Shredded Cheese
  • 1 Baking Dish
  • (Optional) Seasoning to taste (Salt, Pepper, etc)

Instructions:

  1. Pre-heat oven to 350 F
  2. Prepare Elbow Macaroni
    • If package says "5-8" minutes, cook for 5. Macaroni will finish cooking in oven.
  3. Pour macaroni into baking dish
  4. In pot, heat heavy whipping cream till just boiling/bubbling
  5. Gradually mix in shredded cheese until smooth
    • (Optional) Mix in seasonings
  6. Pour cheese mixture over macaroni in baking dish and mix
  7. Put baking dish into oven for 20-25 minutes
    • (Optional) About 5 minutes before end, add toasted bread crumbs to top
  8. Take out of oven and cool for 2-3 minutes before serving

*Add in sauteed/stir-fried protein and vegetables to macaroni for more flavorful combinations
